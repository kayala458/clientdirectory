Learn more about the applications each user may have access to.

  • Website
    Users with access to the Cambridge Associates website can log in to cambridgeassociates.com. Upon logging in, they will have access to several additional resources, such as our Insights Library, which is a collection of Cambridge’s regularly published research reports. The most recent publications are displayed on the user's page upong logging in, but the user can also search for specific items.
  • CA Answers, CA Insights, and Market Matters
    Users with access to these items receive Cambridge Assoicates Research publications via email with regular frequency.

    CA Answers:
    Answers to frequently asked client questions about market action and the market environment in a few paragraphs. Delivered twice a month.

    CA Insights:
    This publication is distributed each Monday afternoon. The CA Insights announces new research publications since the last email that was sent out to the user. Research publications include items such as Endowments Quarterly (which shows data on our Endowments and Foundations clients), Hedge Fund Updaets, and Investment Pool Returns.

    Market Matters:
    This short piece is intended as a high level overview of the key market moves in the month, with a few topical charts (at quarter end) and a brief look at index performance in major currencies. Delivered on a monthly basis.
  • Optica Research - Private and Public Investments
    Optica Research is Cambridge’s Global Research offering. With it, our clients can access manager information and Cambridge insights across the Hedge Fund, Long Only and Private Investment asset classes. More specifically, users can access our full Investment and Operational Due Diligence Reports, Commentary from meetings we’ve had with managers and underlying manager-specific data. On the public side that includes performance, Assets Under Management, exposures, and portfolio characteristics and on the private side that includes IRRs, quartiles, multiples, and Cambridge benchmark figures.
  • Optica Benchmarks
    Optica Benchmarks is Cambridge Associates’ (CA) private investments benchmarking tool. It is a web-based application that allows users to “slice and dice” across the robust fund and investment-level data in CA’s private performance database.

    Optica Benchmarks provides users with the ability to access the official CA Benchmarks and also create customized fund-level benchmarks by filtering on fund asset class geography, vintage year, fund sequence, industry focus, size, IRR range, status, and quartile.
  • Optica Peers
    Optica Peers is a web-based application. Within it, users can view a wide range of metrics, including clients’ historic quarterly performance, clients’ asset allocations, and their spending rates. It’s modeled with a “give and take” approach, so if a client agrees to participate and allow us to share their quarterly data…they can in turn log in and see how they compare to other participating peers.

    Right now, the only clients who are eligible to participate are US –based institutions. That primarily includes Colleges, Universities, Foundations, Hospitals, Museums, etc.
  • PIOS and MMOS
    These applications are similar to Optica Research in that they provide manager's investment performance. The primary difference is that these applications do not offer the user access to Cambridge Associates' research content on the managers. PIOS, or Private Investment One Stop displays only Private Investment manager data and is akin to Private Investments Optica Research. MMOS, or Marketable Manager One Stop displays only Public Investment manager data and is akin to Public Investments Optica Resarch.
